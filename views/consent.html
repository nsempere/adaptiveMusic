Informed Consent Agreement

Please consider this information carefully before deciding whether to participate in this research.

Purpose of Research - We are doing research to understand the effect of music on cognition.

 What you will do in this study - If you decide to participate, you will first be asked to complete a brief questionnaire regarding basic demographic information and musical preferences. Once you are finished, the website will redirect you to a page that explains a cognitive task. Once you understand how to perform this task, the website will play music into your headphones as you perform the task.

 Time Required - The session will take approximately 20-40 minutes to complete.

Risks and Discomfort - While most people find the procedures listed above painless, some report that there is a bit of discomfort associated with paying attention for extended amounts of time. You may refuse to answer any questions or perform any tasks that make you feel uncomfortable at any point during the study.

Compensation - You will receive $0.25/15 minutes via the Amazon Payment System.

Benefits - There are no direct benefits to you aside from the educational experience of participating in the study. However, we expect that the results of this study will add to the body of knowledge on how to design music that facilitates work.

 Confidentiality - Your responses will be recorded. No identifying features (name, face, etc) will be present in the recorded data. This information will be kept strictly confidential and used only for research purposes. The documentation will be identifiable only by a randomly assigned ID number. When research results are reported, responses will be aggregated and described in summary.

 Participation and Withdrawal - Your participation is completely voluntary. Should you decide at any time during the study that you no longer wish to participate, you may discontinue your participation.
